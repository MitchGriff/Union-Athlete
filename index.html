Our Mission

Union Athlete specializes in fitness programming and coaching designed to forge unbreakable athletes.

Creating Union Athlete was an opportunity to bring the best of what our coaching staff does from an athlete development standpoint together with our simple philosophy: Design a plan of attack for each athlete, help expose and overcome weaknesses, and outwork the competition. This process forces athletes to get comfortable with being uncomfortable, and ultimately produces the greatest amount of growth for them. Like the anvil logo, we serve as the foundation for each athlete to face the fire of daily training, and help forge them into something stronger and sharper than they were before. Our motto is Nil Sine Labore, translates as Nothing without labor. Union Athlete embodies the working class mentality when it comes to training and competing. When the lights come on we'll be prepared to win, and when the dust settles you can bet we'll still be standing.

What We Offer

Online Programming:

Intelligently designed athlete programming delivered daily using TrueCoach Personal Training Software. This option can be completed from anywhere in the world, and is geared towards getting serious athletes started towards maximizing their potential, destroying their weaknesses, and performing at their best when it counts.

Off-Site Coaching:

Work directly with a Union Athlete coach and receive personalized fitness programming, designed exclusively for you with monthly consults to help keep you on track. This option is for athletes who need custom programming, nutrition and lifestyle accountability, as well as weekly mindset development, but for proximity reasons, cannot train on-site.

On-Site Coaching:

The Gold standard for Union Athlete development. Complete daily custom programming and maximize your performance by training directly with your coach each day. Meet monthly to monitor progress, evaluate and modify nutrition as needed, develop an unbreakable mindset, assess weaknesses, and build out a monthly plan of attack for taking your game to the next level.

Our Coaches

Cody Loeffler

Cody is a co-founder if Union Athlete. His competitive experience includes 5x CrossFit Games Regionals appearances and 2 trips to the CrossFit Games in 2013 and 2014. He has coached several athletes and teams to CFG regionals, age-group qualifiers, and now coaches CF Sanctional qualifying athletes in the new CFG structure spanning the northeast and east coast. In addition to coaching competitive CrossFit athletes, he has coached and directed strength and conditioning programs at the collegiate and high school levels in Pennsylvania, and has helped a number of athletes achieve division 1 scholarships and have an opportunity to play at the collegiate level.

Greg Strootman

Greg is a co-founder of Union Athlete. He has been active in athletics and specifically playing Ice Hockey since he was six years old. After graduating high school Greg played two years of Junior Hockey before committing to Nichols College in Massachusetts where he played four years of NCAA D-III Hockey. Greg was the Captain his senior year leading his team to a conference championship and the opportunity to play for a national title in the NCAA Tournament. His love for athletics and his time spent training to become a better athlete ultimately led him to pursue a career in training athletes to reach their highest potential. Greg is an enthusiastic and energetic coach with multiple years of experience coaching and programming for individuals of all levels.

Emily Loeffler

Emily is a co-founder of Union Athlete. Her competitive experiences include collegiate volleyball, 5 appearances at the CrossFit Games Regionals, 2 appearances at the CrossFit Games, and 3 years as Captain of the Phoenix Rise in the National Professional Grid League. She has coached many athletes ages 14 and up in volleyball, field hockey, track and field, and CrossFit. With her infectious personality and infectious smile, she loves to build confidence and strength in her athletes!
